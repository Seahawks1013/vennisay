Vennisay is a Christian artist from Lakeland, Florida with a deep-rooted passion for music and an unwavering faith in God. His movement is simple: REAL ART music that's honest, intentional, and rooted in something bigger than the moment.

Formerly in the secular music scene, Vennisay made a transformative journey to redirect his focus toward spreading the message of God's love. REAL ART isn't just a label. It's the standard. Every track he drops is built to last, connect, and mean something.
